How to Join Fortnite Events

Joining a Fortnite event is simple:

  • Mark Your Calendar: Events are announced ahead of time, so stay tuned to Fortnite’s official channels.
  • Log in Early: Popular events fill up quickly. Be ready in the game at least 30 minutes before the event begins.
  • Enjoy the Moment: Immerse yourself and experience the magic as it happens!
