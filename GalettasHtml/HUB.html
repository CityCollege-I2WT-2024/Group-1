Fortnite Battle Royale

Welcome to the Fortnite Battle Royale!

Fortnite Battle Royale is a fast-paced, free-to-play multiplayer game where 100 players drop onto a vast island, battling it out until only one player or team remains standing. Collect resources, build structures, and fight to be the last one left!

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the Battle Royale genre, Fortnite has something for everyone. Jump into action, form squads, and get ready for epic battles with a variety of weapons and gear!
